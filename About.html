About

MEC Groups was founded in 2022 by a group of engineering students. The company was established with the aim of making life easier for people with the help of latest technologies. MEC Groups provide public transport tracking services, this will greatly facilitate life, as well as moving around the city and moving between cities, respectively. MEC Groups Team is working to provide the best routes for the specific times of the day. MEC Groups provide affordable fares to fulfill the requirements of customers of all age groups. At any time the user can find out where their transport is. As a result, knowing the location of the bus would help the user not to waste their time waiting for the bus for several hours. MEC Groups improves its technologies every now and then, and, tries their best to make this world a better place to be in.
