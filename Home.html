What is Intelligent Transportation System?

Intelligent transportation system (ITS) is a sophisticated application that strives to deliver novel services related to various modes of transportation and traffic management, allowing users to be better informed and make safer, more coordinated, and'smarter' use of transportation networks.

The technologies used in intelligent transportation systems range from basic management systems like car navigation, traffic signal control, container management, variable message signs, automatic number plate recognition, or speed cameras, to monitoring applications like security CCTV systems, automatic incident detection, or stopped vehicle detection systems, to more advanced applications that integrate live data and feedback from a variety of other sources. Predictive approaches are also being developed to enable improved modelling and comparison with historical baseline data.
